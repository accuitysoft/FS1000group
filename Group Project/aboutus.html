About Us












Who we are?

Travel Tours company offer small group adventure tours for an adventurous international crowd who want to experience different continents. Our types of tours are the best way to discover various destinations with a unique combination of sightseeing and adventure activities going "where the crowds can't go." We pack adventure and explorations on trips that take you to big cities, National Parks, beaches, small towns and many places away from the tourist routes.

The main draw of a group trip with Travel Tour company is that there’s almost no planning needed on the travelers part. Your destinations and transfers are all sorted for you, so there’s no need to wake up early to hit the bus station for times and tickets, or figure out the best way to cross a border and change money. Our Tour experts handle this all for you, to give you a stress free Holidays. Travel with us and make memories of a lifetime..
